fred tomaselli jack tilton gallery # greene street soho through june # say no to drugs but dont say no to fred tomasellis paintings which include large quantities of drugs chemical and botanical prescription and over the counter legal and illegal mr tomaselli arranges pills stems and leaves into decorative patterns sometimes free standing and sometimes superimposed on painted patterns or landscapes the whole thing is then embedded in a thick layer of transparent resin which from a distance makes the work look strangely like huge glossy illustrations from a textbook or magazine mr tomaselli has been working in this vein for several years and his compositions seem to be getting simultaneously bolder and more subtle the pills have spread out into patterns recalling constellations or molecular bonds while his sierra club landscapes have been reduced to the black trees silhouetted against a twilight sky odds and ends from the past appear with hallucinatory clarity leaves and stems arranged like a real life botanical print or surrounded by blue and red flames recalling psychedelic album covers of the #s the subject is narcotics but the effect is exquisitely controlled pepe karmel
